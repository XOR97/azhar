Right now

I’m in London, on lockdown due to the Covid-19 Coronavirus outbreak. I’m well, but I’m at home to help prevent the spread. This is how I’m spending my time right now.

Current Projects

  • I’m building a UI style guide generator with Eleventy, which takes a set of design tokens stored as yaml data and turns them into Sass variables to use in your styles, and JSON to use in your templates. The idea is inspired by Andy Bell’s Hylia Starter Kit.
  • I just launched Local London, a directory of London food & drink suppliers who deliver to your door. I’m working on the project with my friend Rozzy in response to the Covid-19 outbreak.
  • Ed and I have been working on AIN Jobs, a simple Gatsby powered jobsboard which uses Netlify Forms, Zapier, Airtable and Mailchimp to process data
