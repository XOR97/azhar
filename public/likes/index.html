Likes

This page collects my starred articles in Feedbin. I stole the idea from Robin, who learn’t about it from Dave, who was inspired by this post from Chris.

The final bit of magic I learn’t from Andy. It involves using IFTTT to watch for changes to my Feedbin RSS feed.

So whenever I favourite a new article in Feedbin, IFTTT sends a POST request to Netlify, which then triggers a build hook and pulls in the new articles.
