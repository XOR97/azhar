Design & Code

Hey, I’m Harry Cresswell, a freelance designer and front-end web developer from London, England.

I write articles on design and code and curate a weekly newsletter for designers and front-end web developers. I also keep notes on things I’m learning from books, courses and interesting things I find from various places on the internet.

Currently I live between London and Hamburg, where I work with a handful of clients. In my free time I enjoy running, climbing, hiking and skiing. I practice yoga and meditation most mornings.
